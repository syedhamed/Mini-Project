ROCK PAPER SCISSORS

Instructions
This is Rockpaperscissors game and following are the Instructions to play the game.
The Basic Rules of RPS :
Players deliver hand signals representing rock, paper, or scissors, with the outcome determined by these three rules:
Rock wins against scissors.
The rock will beat scissors every time but will be beaten by paper.
Scissors win against paper.
Scissors are thrown in the same way as rock.
Paper wins against rock.
Paper will beat rock but will be beaten by scissors in no time.
