Game Instructions:

*************************************************************************************************************************************

1. The goal of the game is to form a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line with three of your symbols in a row.

2. The game is played between two players, Player A and Player B.

3. Players take turns to place their symbol in an empty cell by clicking on it.

4. A player wins when they successfully create a line of three of their symbols.

5. If the entire board is filled, and no player has won, the game is a tie.

6. Player A must choose either 'X' or 'O' before starting the game.

7. Click the respective button in the symbol prompt to make the selection.

8. The "Reset" button can be used to start a new game.

**************************************************************************************************************************************
